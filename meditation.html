Step-by-Step Guide to Embark on an Advanced Meditation Journey:

  1. Find a comfortable and upright seated position that allows you to feel at ease and balanced.
  2. Close your eyes gently and softly, embracing a state of serene darkness that allows you to turn inward.
  3. Take slow and deep breaths, inhaling fully to invite a sense of revitalizing energy, and exhaling gradually to release any tension or stress.
  4. Gradually shift your attention to your body, taking a leisurely tour from head to toe, noticing any sensations or feelings that arise along the way.
  5. Remain aware of the thoughts that naturally flow through your mind, simply observing them without judgment or getting caught up in them.
  6. If your mind starts to wander, gently guide it back to the rhythm of your breath, using it as an anchor to bring you back to the present moment.
  7. When you feel ready, softly open your eyes, allowing the external world to gently enter your awareness once again, feeling refreshed and connected.
